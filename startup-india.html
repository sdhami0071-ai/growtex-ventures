Startup India — India's Most Powerful Business Growth Initiative

Startup India is a flagship initiative launched by the Government of India on January 16, 2016. Its goal is simple: to build a strong ecosystem that nurtures innovation and startups in India, driving sustainable economic growth and generating large scale employment opportunities.

At the heart of this initiative is DPIIT Recognition — an official government certification issued by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT). Once recognised, your startup unlocks a suite of powerful benefits that dramatically reduce your tax burden, compliance costs, and legal friction.

In short: DPIIT Recognition is not just a certificate. It is a competitive advantage that puts lakhs of rupees back into your business.

Raise Investor Funding Without the Angel Tax Burden

Under Section 56(2)(VIIB) of the Income Tax Act, investments received by eligible DPIIT startups from accredited investors, non-residents, AIFs (Category I), and listed companies are fully exempt from Angel Tax.
