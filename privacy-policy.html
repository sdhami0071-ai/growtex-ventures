Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

At GrowteX Ventures, accessible from growtex.in, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by GrowteX Ventures and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information about you such as your name, email address, phone number, the contents of the message and/or attachments you may send us, and any other information you may choose to provide.

When you register for an Account or book a call, we may ask for your contact information, including items such as name, company name, address, email address, and telephone number.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our website
  • Understand and analyze how you use our website
  • Develop new products, services, features, and functionality
  • Communicate with you, either directly or through one of our partners
  • Send you emails or WhatsApp messages for booking confirmations

3. Data Protection

We take your data security seriously. All personal information shared via our booking forms or contact portals is encrypted and stored securely. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at legal@growtex.in.
